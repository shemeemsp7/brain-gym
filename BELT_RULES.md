# Karate Belt (Badge) Progression Logic (Updated July 2025)

A challenge is considered **completed** for badge purposes only if its `evaluation` is `"correct"` or `"partially correct"` (as determined by AI feedback).

## Belt Levels

- **White Belt:**  
  - 0 completed challenges (default for all users)

- **Yellow Belt:**  
  - 1–4 completed challenges  
  - All must have `evaluation` = "correct" or "partially correct" (any difficulty)

- **Orange Belt:**  
  - 5–9 completed challenges  
  - All must have `evaluation` = "correct" or "partially correct" (any difficulty)

- **Green Belt:**  
  - 10–19 completed challenges  
  - At least 2 must be `difficulty` = "medium" and `evaluation` = "correct" or "partially correct"

- **Blue Belt:**  
  - 20–29 completed challenges  
  - At least 5 must be `difficulty` = "medium" and `evaluation` = "correct" or "partially correct"

- **Purple Belt:**  
  - 30–39 completed challenges  
  - At least 10 must be `difficulty` = "medium" and at least 2 must be `difficulty` = "advanced"  
  - All must have `evaluation` = "correct" or "partially correct"

- **Brown Belt:**  
  - 40–49 completed challenges  
  - At least 15 must be `difficulty` = "medium" and at least 5 must be `difficulty` = "advanced"  
  - All must have `evaluation` = "correct" or "partially correct"

- **Black Belt:**  
  - 50+ completed challenges  
  - At least 20 must be `difficulty` = "medium" and at least 10 must be `difficulty` = "advanced"  
  - All must have `evaluation` = "correct" or "partially correct"
